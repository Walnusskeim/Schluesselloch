My medals

These medals aren't really special ones, but still nice to have.

Austro-Hungarian medal for Bravery

There have been diffrent variations of this medal over the time. The obverse and reverse changing, when there was a new emperor. This medals obverse shows emperor Charles I looking to the right side. The reverse reads "FORTITVDINI" which is latin for "Der Tapferkeit"

Anniversary commemorative medal for civil servants

This is the 50 year anniversary medal of Emperor Franz Josef' accession to the throne of the Habsburg Empire. The reverse side shows a rectangular shield in the center with the two-line inscription SIGNVM MEMORIAE (Erinnerungszeichen), as well as the years MDCCCXLLVIII-MDCCCXCVIII (1848–1898) in the upper half.

60 year anniversary cross for civil servants

Not a really spectacular medal. It was intended for all persons who were at least 21 years old on December 2, 1908, and who were active civil servants. Non-active civil servants had to have been members of the civil service between 1898 and 1908 and also have at least ten years of active service to qualify for an award.
